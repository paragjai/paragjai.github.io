I am a grad student in computer science at Mila and University of Montreal where my program supervisor is Prof. Yoshua Bengio. Previously, I spent a few amazing years as a Research intern and Research mentor at Microsoft Innovation Lab, where I worked under the supervision of Dr. Srinivas A. I also interned at Center for Data Science and Applied Machine Learning. At CDSAML, I worked under Dr. Shylaja S S on generating text for images (multi-modal text generation).

I have prior experience of applying Machine Learning in the domain of health care. I have also extensively worked in the space of Digital Image Processing and Computer Vision. My current areas of interest involves designing systems and solutions using multi-modal machine learning techniques (primarily, text and image) for real-world problems observed at large-scale.

Keywords: large language models, multi-modal learning, unsupervised learning, supervised learning, reinforcement learning

I graduated with B.Tech in Computer Science from PES University, India in 2018. For more details about my background, refer to my CV. If you'd like to chat with me about my work or research in general, feel free to reach out!
